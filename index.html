Urban Adamah – Farming for Faith & Community

Video is a powerful communications tool. This example tells the story of Urban Adamah, located a small plot of land in the midst of densely populated Berkeley, California. Through its fellowship program for young adults, Urban Adamah is nurturing Judaism while teaching the principles of farming and providing fresh produce to those who need it most.
